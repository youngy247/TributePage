Steven Gerrard

The longest Liverpool FC captain ever

Steven Gerrard is a retired professional footballer who is best known for his time at Liverpool FC, where he spent 17 seasons as captain and led the team to numerous victories, including the Champions League title in 2005. Steven Gerrard is a retired professional footballer who is best known for as being a Liverpool FC legend. Having spent the majority of his playing career at the club. He made over 700 appearances for the Reds across 17 seasons as captain, scoring over 180 goals and winning numerous trophies, including the infamous Champions League, FA Cup, and the UEFA Cup.
