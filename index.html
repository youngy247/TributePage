Steven Gerrard

The longest Liverpool FC captain ever

Steven Gerrard is a retired professional footballer who is best known for as being a Liverpool FC legend. Having spent the majority of his playing career at the club from 1998 to 2015. He made 504 Premier League appearances for the Reds across 12 seasons (2003-2015) as captain, scoring over 180 goals and winning numerous trophies, including the infamous Champions League, FA Cup, and the UEFA Cup.
He also captained england 38 times and got 114 caps for his country.
